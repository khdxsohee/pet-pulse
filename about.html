Our Mission: Connecting Hearts

Learn more about Pet Pulse and our dedication to animal welfare.

Who We Are

Pet Pulse is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned, neglected, and abused animals. Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate animal lovers, we believe every animal deserves a loving and safe forever home. We work tirelessly to provide a second chance for these deserving creatures.

Our Vision

To create a world where every pet has a loving home, is treated with compassion, and is free from suffering. We envision a community where responsible pet ownership is the norm and animal welfare is prioritized.

What We Do

  • **Rescue:** We actively rescue animals from high-kill shelters, abusive situations, and abandonment.
  • **Rehabilitate:** We provide necessary veterinary care, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and behavioral training to prepare them for adoption.
  • **Rehome:** We meticulously screen potential adopters to ensure a perfect match between pets and families.
  • **Educate:** We advocate for responsible pet ownership, pet-friendly communities, and animal welfare through public awareness campaigns.
  • **Support:** We offer ongoing support and resources to adoptive families.
